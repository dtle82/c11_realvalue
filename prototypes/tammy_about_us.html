About This App

Location is the key to real estate. Finally, there is a place where you can add up all the little things that makes a neighborhood desirable. Whether you're looking for the best cup of coffee in town, or the closest distance to work, you get to choose what's most important to you. Tell us your priorities when searching for an ideal location for your new home, and we will do the work for you. Simply check the boxes, and a score will be calculated based on your choices.
